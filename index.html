Introduction

Aitra ("we", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Aitra mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

1. Accessibility Service Data

Aitra uses Android Accessibility Service to provide screen translation:

  • Data Collected: Only the text you select on your screen when you request a translation
  • Purpose: To translate the text to your target language
  • Storage: Translated texts are NOT permanently stored on your device or our servers
  • Processing Time: Text is only temporarily processed during translation and immediately deleted

IMPORTANT: The app does not read, store, or share any content from your screen without your explicit permission.

2. Authentication

  • Anonymous ID: An anonymous user ID is created using Firebase Authentication
  • Purpose: Spam protection and preventing service abuse
  • Personal Information: Your name, email, or phone number is NOT required and not collected

3. Usage Statistics

Currently, no analytics or usage statistics are collected.

How We Use Information

Collected information is used only for:

  1. Providing Translation Service: To translate selected text to your target language
  2. Service Quality: To improve translation results
  3. Security: To prevent spam and abuse

Data Security
